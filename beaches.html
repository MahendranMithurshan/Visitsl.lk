Beaches

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay's lengthy ribbon of beach, partially shaded by coconut palms, is reason enough to visit. Its allure to surfers is clear during the season, but it is simply a great location to relax and rest all year. A active fishing hamlet may be found on the southern side of the beach, with wooden shacks jumbled together at the top of the sand for the hard-working employees. Residents congregate here at evening for a conversation and a paddle.

Marble Beach

Marble Beach, also known as Marble Bay, is one of Trincomalee's most stunning beaches. Because it is 17 kilometers outside of Trincomalee, few people are aware of its existence. At the north end of Marble Beach's cove, there is a resort and restaurant run by the air force. Spend your time towards the south end of the beach, where the sea is magnificent and the environment is quiet. This is, in our opinion, one of Sri Lanka's best beaches! Unawatuna Beach

Unawatuna Beach is certain to figure on any list of Sri Lanka's top beaches. This beach, located about an hour from Colombo, Sri Lanka's capital city, is a beach lover's paradise. Unawatuna beach is a terrific place to unwind and get away from the usual, with elegant restaurants, pubs, shacks, beach houses, retail lanes, and exciting activities to partake in in the surrounding areas. The beach features golden sand and lovely water and is shaped like a banana. The lush palm trees here not only add to the beauty of the beach, but also help to keep the environment comfortable on most days. On a visit here, one is guaranteed to encounter small children from local families taking a dip.
