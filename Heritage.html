Heritage

Temple of Tooth

The sacred Buddhist site of Senkadagalapura, also known as the city of Senkadagalapura, was the last capital of the Sinhala rulers, whose patronage allowed the Dinahala civilization to flourish for over 2,500 years until the British occupied Sri Lanka in 1815. It is also home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic (the Buddha's precious tooth), which is a popular pilgrimage destination.

Adam's Peak

Adam's Peak is a holy peak in the heart of Sri Lanka. All religions appear to come together on this mountain: Christians and Muslims think here is where Adam made his first step on earth, Buddhists believe Buddha's first footstep was set here, and Hindus believe Shiva took his first step on earth via this peak.
